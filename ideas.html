IDEAS




Sustainable Development

At Vishwakarma Industries, we believe in sustainable development i.e. the development that meets the needs of the present without compromising the ability of future generations to meet their own needs. That is the reason, we use timber wood which comes from genuine sources. We ensure the wood comes from certified plantations which follow the compliances as directed by the NGT( National Green Tribunal). We use only Vriksh Certified Sheesham wood (Dalbergia sisso). The Vriksh Certification is provided by the GICIA India under the guidance of EPCH ( Export Promotion Council of India). We strongly believe - "The world's forests are a shared stolen treasure that we must put back for maintaining the nature's balance."




Buyers Engagement

At Vishwakarma Industries, we focus on customer satisfaction because we believe,-

"A satisfied customer is the best source of advertisement"

Our product development team sits with our buyers for hours until they are satisfied with the product. It helps us to understand the need of the customer. It also helps us understand and develop products according to the trends.

The areas of focus are the material, structure, finish and packaging of the product which are customized according to the buyers. The product undergoes a number of tests and quality checks before leaving our premises. We are constantly in touch with our buyers incase they suggest any changes, alterations or they need any after sales support.




Better Working Environment

At Vishwakarma Industries, we feel - To win the marketplace, you must first win the workplace and it is our strong belief that "Customer satisafaction starts with Employee satisfaction."

"The greatest asset of a company is its people." We respect our employees and provide them a safe and pleasant working environment so that they can achieve their goals. Employees are rewarded and recognized for their efforts and achievements.

We make them feel relaxed at workplace by conducting playful and creative activities as we believe - "Don't build a company, build a family."

Child Labour is strictly not allowed in our factories which is prohibited according to the compliances.




Attention To Details

At Vishwakarma Industries, we put in special efforts for the attention to minute details which has become our USP (Unique Selling Point).

As said by John Wooden,

"It is the little things that are vital. Little things make big things happen."

The amount of work we do may be not huge but the attention, we give to each order is surely our plus point. We always try to work for that extra detail which can be added to make a product better and even better. We try to offer our buyers, the best woodwork and best finishes.

We strongly believe - "It is the attention to detail that makes the difference between average and stunning."
